Corridos

Natanael cano

Natanael Rubén Cano Monge, known as Natanael Cano, is a rapper and singer-songwriter of the Mexican regional genre. He is the creator of the concept of corridos tumbados, the name of one of his albums. His music fuses regional Mexican music genres such as corrido and sierreño with rap.

Junior H

Luis Antonio Herrera Perez, better known as Junior H, is a Mexican singer, rapper, composer and musician of the genre of lying corridos, a fusion of regional Mexican music such as corrido and sierreño-banda with trap.

Ovi

Ovidio Guzmán, better known by his stage name Ovi, is a Cuban rapper and singer-songwriter of corrido, reggaeton and Latin trap.

Trap

Paulo londra

is an Argentine rapper, singer and songwriter who emerged from freestyle competitions in the plaza that were held in an amateur way, such as El Quinto Escalón, which suddenly became one of the most popular competitions in Argentina and Latin America, and where Londra began to gain recognition. In 2017, he released his first single, "Relax", which was an immediate success. Doing a duet with producer Ovy on The Drums, Paulo released the single "Condenado para el Millon", which marked his worldwide breakthrough. In 2018 he became the Argentine artist with the most views on Spotify and YouTube

Duki

Mauro Ezequiel Lombardo, artistically known as Duki, is an Argentine rapper and composer, considered the leading voice of trap music in Argentina, due to his multiple successes with his singles and his particular and distinctive voice and staging.

Bad bunny

Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, artistically known as Bad Bunny, is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer, songwriter and actor. His style of music is generally defined as Latin trap and reggaeton, but he has also performed other genres such as rock, kizomba and soul.

Rock

Guns N Roses

Guns N' Roses is an American hard rock band formed in Hollywood on the Sunset Strip area around Santa Monica in the Los Angeles, California metropolitan area in 1985. The group was founded by vocalist Axl Rose and guitarist Izzy Stradlin.

Artick Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys are a British rock band, formed in Sheffield, United Kingdom. The group consists of lead guitarist and vocalist Alex Turner, guitarist Jamie Cook, drummer Matt Helders, and bassist Nick O'Malley.

Los Apson

Los Apson are a Mexican rock and roll group from Agua Prieta, Sonora. It was one of the first successful Mexican rock and roll musical groups of the second half of the 60's in Mexico.
